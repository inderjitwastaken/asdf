Our Mission

We aim to drastically increase the safety of ski resorts for both customers and staff by implementing our newly innovated Drone Cell into North American ski resorts. The core undertaking of the Drone Cell is to assist the Ski Patrol with their duties of keeping a safe ski area; ultimately making it safer for everyone. We use our advaced drone technology and combine it with the operations of ski patrol to make resorts safer.

What we Do

The Drone Cell is a professional drone team consisting of 3 trained and certified drone pilots who also obtain National Ski Patrol's (NSP) Outdoor Emergency Care certification or national Ski Patrol's (NSP) Outdoor Emergency Care certification. Each and every ski resort has a Ski Patrol, a team of patrollers who work tirelessly to secure the ski area, maintain critical equipment and infrastructure, and rescue skiers in distress. They are crucial to the operation of these resorts, but implementing our Drone Cell will only help elevate the Ski Patrol’s operations.
